Notes to Grader: At the bottom of the page
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
Controls:
  • Orbit Control: move camera around, zoom in/out
  • GUI Control Widget: Several controls for the camera and four different lights
Notes:
  • Castle is made with primary shapes - cube, cone, cylinder; has animated icosahedron gems within the inner boundary; and is placed inside a skybox
  • Number of shapes used in the castle: 20 (five towers) + (8 + 19 x 4) (two boundary walls) + 7 gems inside
  • The center of the castle is slightly shifted away from origin to [-20, -20, -20]. All coordinates in the controls are with respect to the actual origin
  • Has 3 light sources: ambient, directional, and spotlight with some controls
  • Spotlight is focused on one of the gems
  • To see the effect of any light (e.g. spotlight) properly. minimize the intensity of other lights
  • Castle walls have stone texture (from https://cc0-textures.com/t/cc0t-bricks-008
  • Custom textured 3D model(s) (obj) included: lion head (from https://polyhaven.com/a/lion_head by Tina)
  • Extra 1: Fog - can be turned on/off
  • Extra 2: Shadows of the gems from the directional light. Changing the light's position (i.e. direction) changes the location and size of the shadows
  • Extra 3: Can pick a gem and change its color by clicking on it; clicking on it again restores its color
