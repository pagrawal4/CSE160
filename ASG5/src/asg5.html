Notes to Grader: At the bottom of the page
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
Controls:
  • Orbit Control: move camera around, zoom in/out
  • GUI Control Widget: Several controls for the camera and four different lights
Notes:
  • Castle is made with primary shapes - cube, cone, cylinder; has animated icosahedron gems inside; and is inside a skybox
  • Number of shapes used in the castle: 20 (five towers) + (8 + 19 x 4) (two boundary walls) + 7 gems inside
  • Center of the castle is slightly shifted away from origin to [-20, -20, -20]
  • Has 4 light sources: ambient, directional, point, spotlight with some controls
  • Castle walls have stone texture (from https://cc0-textures.com/t/cc0t-bricks-008)
  • Custom textured 3D model(s) (obj) included: lion head (from https://polyhaven.com/a/lion_head by Tina)
  • Extra 1: Fog
  • Extra 2: Reflection of the gems from point light
  • Extra 3: Picking a gem changes its color, clicking on it again restores it's color
