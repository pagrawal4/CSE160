Light Position: X Y Z Light Color: Ambient Diffuse Specular

Field of View

Controls:
  • WASD: move forward/left/backward/right
  • QE: pan left/right
  • Mouse drag: look around
  • Light ON/OFF: toggle light on/off. Turning light off removes the lights effects (works like Assignment 3)
  • Light Animation ON/OFF: toggle light animation on/off
  • Turning on Spotlight turns off the other light
  • Robot Animation ON/OFF: toggle robot animation on/off
  • Robot Moonwalk Animation ON/OFF: toggle robot moonwalk on/off
  • R: reset camera
Notes:
  • Sky, grass, and homes are not shiny and have no specular component
  • Ball, cube, and robot are shiny and have specular component
  • Changing light position will stop light animation
  • Space for adding/removing blocks is highlighted
  • The plot that a home sits on is highlighted
  • New home that doesn't have enough space at highlighted space will not be placed
