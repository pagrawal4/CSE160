Light Position: X Y Z Light Color: Ambient Diffuse Specular

Field of View

Controls:
  • WASD: move forward/left/backward/right
  • QE: pan left/right
  • Mouse drag: look around
  • Robot can be animated to walk normally or moonwalk
  • R: reset camera
Notes:
  • Sky, grass, and homes are not shiny and have no specular component
  • Ball, cube, and robot are shiny and have specular component
  • Turning light off removes the lights effects (shows like Assignment 3)
  • Light animation stops when light position is changed, but can be restarted
