Notes to Grader: At the bottom of the page
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Field of View:

Explore:
  • WASD: move forward/left/backward/right
  • QE: pan left/right
  • Mouse drag: look around
  • Plus (+): add block at highlighted space
  • Minus (-): remove block from highlighted space
  • H: add home at highlighted space
  • J: remove home at highlighted space
  • R: reset world
  • Humanoid robot: walks normally or moonwalks
  • Animation: can be toggled on/off
Notes:
  • One texture used for grass, four for homes
  • Sky and robot use solid colors
  • Highlighted space for adding/removing blocks uses grass texture combined with solid color
  • Each added home gets a different texture from the set of four textures
  • The plot that a home sits on is highlighted
  • New home that doesn't have enough space at highlighted space will not be placed
  • Story: The motivation for my Blocky World comes from all the homes destroyed by the recent wildfires in California as well as a persistent lack of housing at UCSC and in California in general that has left many people homeless. Together, we can build communities!
